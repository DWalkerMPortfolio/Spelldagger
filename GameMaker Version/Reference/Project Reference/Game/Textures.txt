- obj_control
	- create:
		- level data initialization:
			- texture grid initialized
			- texture sheet loaded
	- step begin:
		- sector management (when sector changed):
			- player texture grid loaded
			- player low texture grid loaded
	- pre-draw:
		- calculate textures:
			- texture surface created (if sector changed)
	- draw begin:
		- draw textures + calculate shadows:
			- texture surface drawn (including flicker effect)
			- dark texture surface calculated (if lights or sector changed)
			- lights subtracted from dark texture surface (if lights or sector changed)
			- dark texture surface drawn
